How to Integrate the Treatment Recommender Widget

Step 1: Set Up EmailJS for Your Med Spa

Each med spa should create their own EmailJS account to comply with HIPAA and maintain control of patient data:

  1. Create an account at EmailJS for your med spa
  2. Set up a service connected to your med spa's email address
  3. Create an email template with parameters: {firstName}, {lastName}, {email}, {phone}, {newsletter}, {treatmentPlan}, and {timestamp}
  4. Note your EmailJS Service ID, Template ID, and User ID for configuration

Important: For healthcare providers dealing with patient information, ensure your EmailJS setup complies with relevant privacy regulations like HIPAA.

Step 2: Include the Treatment Recommender Script

Add the compiled JavaScript to your HTML:

<script src="path/to/treatment-recommender.js"></script>

Step 3: Configure EmailJS

Configure the EmailJS service with your med spa's credentials:


<script>
  // Set up EmailJS configuration with your med spa's credentials
  window.TreatmentRecommender.configureEmailService({
    serviceId: 'YOUR_EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID',
    templateId: 'YOUR_EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID',
    userId: 'YOUR_EMAILJS_USER_ID'
  });
</script>

Step 4: Customize the Widget for Your Med Spa

Customize the widget appearance and content for your med spa:


<script>
  // Customize for your specific med spa
  window.TreatmentRecommender.configureSiteSettings({
    title: "YOUR MED SPA NAME",
    subtitle: "Find your perfect treatment",
    aboutUsText: "Your med spa's custom about text here.",
    contactInfo: {
      email: "contact@yourmedspa.com",
      phone: "(555) 555-5555",
      address: "123 Med Spa Street, City",
    },
    navigation: {
      showAbout: true,
      showTreatments: true,
      showIntegrationGuide: false, // Hide for client sites
    },
    copyrightName: "Your Med Spa Name",
  });
</script>

Step 5: Add the Treatment Recommender to Your Page

Insert the recommender widget in your desired location:


<div id="treatment-recommender-container"></div>

<script>
  // Render the Treatment Recommender component
  ReactDOM.render(
    React.createElement(window.TreatmentRecommender.default),
    document.getElementById('treatment-recommender-container')
  );
</script>

HIPAA Considerations

For Med Spas: As a healthcare provider, you should take these additional steps:

  1. Use your own EmailJS account: Each med spa should manage their own EmailJS account and patient data.
  2. Review EmailJS security: Ensure EmailJS's security practices align with your HIPAA compliance needs.
  3. Consider Business Associate Agreement: You may need a BAA with EmailJS depending on your specific situation.
  4. Secure data handling: Establish proper procedures for handling the treatment plan data once received.
  5. Privacy policy: Update your website privacy policy to include information about the treatment recommender.

Always consult with a healthcare compliance expert to ensure your implementation meets all necessary privacy requirements.

Note: This setup ensures each med spa manages their own EmailJS configuration and patient data, which helps maintain proper data privacy controls.
